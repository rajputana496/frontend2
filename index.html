Frequently Asked Questions

What diseases can be treated with Panchakarma procedures?

Panchakarma is beneficial in managing chronic conditions like arthritis, digestive disorders, stress, skin problems, respiratory diseases, and metabolic imbalances. It supports the body’s natural healing process.

Who should seek Panchakarma?

Anyone experiencing lifestyle-related health concerns, chronic illnesses, or simply seeking detoxification and rejuvenation can opt for Panchakarma. It is suitable for both preventive and therapeutic care.

Does Panchakarma offer instant relief for health conditions?

Panchakarma is designed for long-term healing rather than quick fixes. While some people may experience immediate lightness and relaxation, the true benefits develop gradually with proper treatment and lifestyle changes.

What will be my diet during Panchakarma treatment and after?

Your diet will be light, easily digestible, and tailored to your body type. Typically, it includes khichdi, herbal teas, and warm fluids. After treatment, gradual reintroduction of a balanced Ayurvedic diet is recommended.

Are Panchakarma procedures only for individuals with diseases?

No. Panchakarma is also recommended for healthy individuals as a preventive and rejuvenative therapy. It helps maintain balance, strengthen immunity, and prevent future ailments.
